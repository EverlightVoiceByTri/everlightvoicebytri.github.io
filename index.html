About Tri

A voice born from music & soul

I'm Tri — a female voiceover artist with a warm, conversational tone and bright, energetic delivery rooted in years of Gospel and R&B/Soul singing. My background as a church and choir singer gives my voice something you can't manufacture: natural breath control, emotional depth, and a rhythm that draws listeners in.

I record from my professional home studio and bring the same dedication to a 15-second commercial as I do to a full-length audiobook. I'm building my voiceover career with passion, discipline, and an absolute refusal to give up.

When I set my mind to something — I succeed.
