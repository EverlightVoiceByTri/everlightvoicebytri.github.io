About Tri

A voice born from music & soul

I'm Tri — a female voiceover artist with a warm, conversational tone and bright, energetic delivery rooted in years of Gospel and R&B/Soul singing. My background as a church and choir singer gives my voice something you can't manufacture: natural breath control, dynamic range, emotional depth, and an instinctive sense of rhythm and pacing.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Digital Media with a concentration in Audio/Video Production — and I bring that same commitment to learning and growing to every voiceover project I take on.

I record from my professional home studio and bring the same dedication to a 15-second commercial as I do to a full-length audiobook. When I set my mind to something — I succeed.

Singer. Student. Storyteller. Building something real.
